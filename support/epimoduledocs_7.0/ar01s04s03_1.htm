AssociationDeleting & AssociationPublishing events

 Top  Previous  Next

AssociationDeleting and AssociationPublishing events are fired just before an association is deleted from between two topics, or an association is about to be published and become viewable to the users of the web site. The association object on the AssociationPublishing event is modifyable, it contains the published association. There is no post-delete or post-publish event.
