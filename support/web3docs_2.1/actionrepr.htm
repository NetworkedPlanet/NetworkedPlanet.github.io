Description

Representation of a single operation within a Transaction.

 

XML Representation

<ActionRepr Id="%action id%" OperationType="%http method name%">

 <Uri>%resource uri%</Uri>

 <ETag>%etag%</ETag>

 <Content>%request content string%</Content>

 <Response Ok="true" ETag="%response etag%">%response body%</Response>

</ActionRepr>

         "Content":"%response body%"

 },

 "OperationType":"%http method name%"

}
